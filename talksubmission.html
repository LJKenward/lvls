About Levels Conf

Levels is a conference for growing technical careers, from the very beginning. We aim to give people a platform to share, grow and connect, regardless of experience level. Levels brings together future industry leaders in a fun and engaging environment.

We are a tech agnostic conference - come at us y’all! Whether you’re front end, back end, mobile, infrastructure, data science, machine learning, or whatevs, you’ve got a home here. Levels is about growing our careers so having a broad understanding of many areas of the software development universe is key!

Talk Suggestions

We have found that the most inspiring, jaw dropping, on the edge of your seat and engaging talks are those that tell a story. This makes the talk relatable and much easier for an audience to empathise with the speaker. This doesn’t mean that we dislike technical talks! We just feel that telling us an engaging story about how you solved a bug at work is much more interesting than simply reciting the 5 steps you take when troubleshooting a problem. So channel your inner Steven Spielberg/Gillian Armstrong/Wayne Blair… heck channel your inner David Cronenberg if that’s your thing.

Learning and inspiration comes in many forms and from a wide range of people. We will be accepting technical and non-technical speakers. No matter your experience level, you are welcome! That being said the majority of our attendees will be towards the junior end of their career. One way to make sure your talk is junior accessible is to provide context around your topic, and not assume several years experience in it. Also, as we are a language agnostic conference, it’s worth taking this into account when crafting your talk.

We’d love to have talk submissions in the follow areas (in no particular order):

  • Front end
  • Back end
  • Mobile
  • Dev ops
  • Tools
  • CSS
  • Your journey
  • Essential skills
  • Career development

We’re super excited to be inviting submissions from interstate and New Zealand speakers. We will be flying in and providing 4 nights accomodation for any speakers outside of Victoria. As this is our first edition of Levels we’re going to keep applicants close to home this time around so we won’t be accepting applications from international speakers further abroad.

All successful applicants will, of course, be given 1 free ticket to Levels.

What happens after you submit

Submissions close midnight Tuesday 24nd April. All applicants will be emailed within two weeks of the submission deadline. This email will include next steps and further information.
